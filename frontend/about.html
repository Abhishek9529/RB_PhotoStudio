Studio story

From small portrait sessions to complete visual experiences.

Founded in Mudana by Rushi Bhutekar, RB Photo Studio began as a boutique portrait space and grew into a full-service photography brand for weddings, families, brands and events. Our process is calm, organized and detail focused, so clients enjoy the shoot as much as the final gallery.

Every project starts with a creative brief and ends with a refined set of images prepared for albums, prints, websites and social platforms.
